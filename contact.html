For Commissions

I'm primarily a landscape painter, focusing on, but not limited to, skyscapes and starscapes (as you will see if you browse through my website). The minimum size painting that I can produce is 40cm x 40cm, however if you would like something smaller please let me know and I will see what I can do!

Every sky holds a story. I paint the stars and clouds as they appeared in the moments that matter most to you - whether it's your wedding day, anniversary, birth of a child, or any milestone that deserves to be remembered in the stars.
